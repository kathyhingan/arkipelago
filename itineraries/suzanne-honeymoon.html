Journey One

El Nido to Coron, Privately

December 22–25, 2026 · 4 Days, 3 Nights · 2 Pax

A crossing built around distance from other people, not distance covered. El Nido is experienced from the water only, never the town, and the route north into Coron opens onto shipwreck diving and a lake unlike anything else in the country. This is the fixed centrepiece of the trip, the dates Suzanne already gave us, and it sits inside all three options for the fuller journey below.
