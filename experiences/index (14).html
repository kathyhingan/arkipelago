Private Island Buyout
Exclusive access to a private island property in the Calamian Group (Palawan) or Siargao outer islands. Your team is the only group. No other guests. Complete privacy for the duration. Available from 3 nights.

Gala Dinner on the Beach
Private dinner on an uninhabited beach or sandbar — no other boats in sight. Custom menu, private bar, candlelit setup. Structured as the anchor experience of the retreat. Arrives at sunset. Ends when you decide.

Diving & Wreck Experiences
WWII Japanese shipwrecks in Coron — among the top ten dive sites in the world. Intro courses for non-divers run simultaneously. Both groups meet at the surface. Suitable as a half-day experience within a structured programme.

Island-Hopping & Water Days
Private vessel, private crew, private schedule. Kayangan Lake at dawn, Twin Lagoon by kayak, uninhabited sandbars at midday. Designed as a team experience — the kind of day that generates shared references for years.

Bukidnon or the Cordillera highlands — for groups who want something beyond the archipelago. Cool air, vast plateaus, indigenous cultural experiences. Unusual enough to be genuinely memorable. Rarely offered by any other operator.

Where we take corporate groups

Four destinations.
One extraordinary programme.

Each destination below works as a standalone retreat or as part of a multi-destination journey. We recommend based on group size, programme objectives, and your team's flight origin.

Mimaropa · Palawan
Coron & the Calamian Islands
Our primary recommendation for leadership retreats and incentive travel. Private island buyouts in the Calamian Group. WWII wreck diving. Kayangan Lake — the clearest lake in Asia. Completely private island-hopping. The most dramatic setting in the Arkipelago portfolio.
Best for: senior leadership · island buyouts · 4–6 days
Caraga · Mindanao
Siargao
Smaller leadership groups. Private mangrove lagoons, Sugba Lagoon, Sohoton Cove — the bioluminescent jellyfish experience at night is genuinely unlike anything else in the region. The outer islands between Siargao and Bucas Grande see almost no visitors.
Best for: leadership teams of 6–14 · 3–5 days
Oriental Mindoro · Near Manila
Puerto Galera
Two hours from Manila — the most practical short-retreat option for Manila-based multinationals (BGC, Makati, Ortigas). World-class diving. Private coves. Quiet Aninuan Beach. A 3-day retreat from BGC is genuinely viable with no domestic flights required.
Best for: Manila-based teams · 2–4 days
Ilocos Region · Northern Luzon
Vigan & Ilocos Heritage Route
The cultural retreat option — for leadership teams who want depth over beach. UNESCO colonial heritage, private heritage house dinners, the Bangui Windmills coastline. Paired with Baguio and the Cordillera highlands for a 5–7 day cultural leadership journey.
Best for: cultural programmes · senior retreats · 5–7 days

Common questions

What corporate clients ask us.

What group sizes do you handle?
We design corporate retreats for groups of 6 to 30 people. For groups larger than 30, we assess on a case-by-case basis — certain private island properties can accommodate more with advance planning. Group size determines destination and accommodation options, which we discuss in the first briefing call.
How does a Philippines corporate retreat compare to the Maldives or Bali?
The Philippines delivers a comparable luxury island experience to the Maldives at significantly lower cost per head — typically 40–60% less for equivalent private island access. Additionally: no visa required for most nationalities, direct flights from Singapore and Hong Kong under 3.5 hours, and extraordinary variety — you can move from a private island to a UNESCO heritage city within one itinerary. The Philippines also offers cultural depth neither the Maldives nor Bali can match at this level.
Can you support hybrid sessions and AV requirements?
Yes. We coordinate AV and connectivity requirements as part of the brief — projectors, screens, microphone systems, dedicated WiFi upgrades, and hybrid video conferencing setup. We document requirements in advance and verify capability at each property before confirming. For remote island locations where connectivity is limited, we advise on this clearly during planning.
Who is the point of contact during the retreat itself?
One dedicated Arkipelago contact, available 24/7 for the full duration of your retreat. The same person who managed the brief manages the on-ground execution. You are not handed off. For the event manager or EA who organised this, that means one number to call if anything needs adjusting — and nothing should need adjusting, because we've already planned for the scenarios.
What are the best Philippines destinations for corporate retreats?
Coron and the Calamian Islands in Palawan are our primary recommendation for leadership retreats and incentive travel — private island buyouts, WWII wreck diving, extraordinary seascapes. Puerto Galera is the practical option for Manila-based teams: 2 hours from BGC, no domestic flight required. Siargao works well for smaller leadership groups. We recommend based on group size, programme objectives, and flight logistics from your team's base.
