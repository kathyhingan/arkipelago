Where we take couples

The most romantic corners of the archipelago

These are not the destinations you'll find on every list. They are the ones we recommend because we have seen what they do to people — the silence, the colour, the feeling of being somewhere no guidebook sent you.

Palawan · Mimaropa
Coron & Port Barton
Kayangan Lake at dawn — the clearest lake in Asia, reached by a short karst hike with nobody else in sight. WWII shipwrecks for divers. Uninhabited islands for everyone else. Port Barton is what El Nido was twenty years ago.
7–10 days recommended
Caraga Region · Mindanao
Siargao
Beyond the surf break — private mangrove lagoons accessible only by small boat at high tide, Guyam Island on a sandbar that appears on no official map, Sohoton Cove at night with bioluminescent jellyfish. Extraordinary.
5–8 days recommended
Pamalican Island
Amanpulo
Forty-five minutes by private Cessna from Manila. One of the finest resort properties in Southeast Asia — and by design, no mass tourism problem. For proposals, honeymoons, or simply the most private week of your lives. From USD 1,200/night.
3–7 nights standalone

Common questions

Things couples ask us

Can you plan a surprise proposal in the Philippines?
Yes — and it's one of our specialities. We handle the location, photographer positioning, decoy activity, timing, and contingency plan. Your partner will not suspect a thing. We have planned proposals across Coron, Palawan, Siargao and Amanpulo. Every single one has worked.
What makes a Philippines honeymoon different from the Maldives or Bali?
The Philippines offers something the Maldives cannot — variety. In one journey you can move from the shipwrecks and karst lakes of Coron to the mangrove lagoons of Siargao to a private island with no other guests. The water is the same colour. The privacy is comparable. The stories are not.
Do you arrange private villas and overwater accommodation?
Yes. We work with the finest private villa operators across Palawan, Siargao, and the Visayas, as well as Amanpulo on Pamalican Island — one of the most private resort properties in Southeast Asia. All accommodation is confirmed after we understand exactly what you want.
We want to go somewhere no one else goes. Is that possible?
That is precisely what we design. Guyam Island in Siargao, Kayangan Lake in Coron at 6am, German Island off Port Barton — these are places where the only boat in sight is yours. We know where they are, and we know what time to be there.
How far in advance should we plan?
For honeymoons, six to twelve months is ideal — particularly if you want Amanpulo or specific villa properties during peak season (December to April). For proposals, we can move faster, but three months gives us the best options. Reach out whenever you're ready — we'll tell you what's possible.
