PALAWAN, PHILIPPINES

Coron

Where Limestone Meets the Deep

Untamed & Entirely Different

Coron is the crown jewel of northern Palawan. Defined by its jagged karst landscapes and deep-water WWII shipwrecks, it offers a dramatic contrast to the beaches of El Nido.

For those seeking a more rugged, private, and ancient connection to the archipelago, Coron is unmatched.

The Highlights

Kayangan Lake

Often cited as the cleanest lake in Asia, accessible via a climb that offers the most iconic view in the Philippines.

Twin Lagoons

A hidden world where saltwater meets freshwater, accessible only through a narrow crevice in the limestone walls.

The Sunken Fleet

Dive or snorkel through twelve Japanese WWII shipwrecks, now transformed into vibrant artificial reefs.
